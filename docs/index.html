My Projects

Bloodsoaked Boots

My main project that is a WIP. It is a weird western ttrpg (tabletop role-playing game) set in an alternate early 1900s North, Central and South America.
Set on the planet Caracol, players don different archtypes of humans as they attempt to live their lives in a harsh and steadily evolving world.
The newly formed 'Federal Government' tightens their iron grip over the people of the country.
Gangs press back using occult means that they steal from ancient orders,worshippers of the devil, and cults that worship the very emnations of god itself.
I plan to eventually host the pdf of my rpg once it's in a more presentable state. Email me if you'd like a copy of the alpha release!
